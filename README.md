# 👋 Hello! This is Xinhe!

I am a lecturer (permanent position) in the **Department of Information and Management Science (社会情報学科)** at **Otaru University of Commerce**（小樽商科大学）, a national university in Hokkaido, Japan. My research lies at the intersection of **Political Science**, **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, and **Social Studies**. I completed my Ph.D. in Information Science at **Graduate School of Information Sciences, Tohoku University** in March 2024.

---

## 🔍 Research Interests

- 📊 Analysis of local government speeches and legislative records  
- 🧠 Natural language processing for political and social phenomena  
- 📌 Visualization of policy positions and discourse structures  
- 🌍 Crisis response communication (e.g., post-disaster governance)  
- 🗣️ Computational social science and transparency of political information
  
---

## 📚 Teaching

I teach courses that bridge **information science** with real-world social issues, including:

- 社会情報論 (Social Informatics)  
- 情報システム論 (Information Systems)  
- システム戦略論 (Systems Strategy)  
- 基礎ゼミナール (Introductory Seminar)  

These classes emphasize practical analytical skills, programming basics, and data-informed social understanding.
